Terms of use

What WhyPayFees is (and isn't)

WhyPayFees is an advertising platform for private property sellers in and around Melbourne, Derbyshire. We publish listings and pass buyer enquiries on to the relevant seller; we are not an estate agent. We do not value properties, conduct viewings, negotiate offers, or handle any money relating to a sale. Once we have passed an enquiry to a seller, all further contact, viewings, negotiation and the resulting sale take place directly between buyer and seller, who should each take their own legal advice.

Seller responsibilities

  • You must be the legal owner of the property, or have the owner's written authority to list it. We may ask for proof of ownership before publishing.
  • Your listing must be accurate and not misleading, including price, tenure, and any material facts a buyer would reasonably need to make a decision.
  • You must hold a valid Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) before your home is marketed, as required by law.
  • The phone number on your listing is published openly. Only include details you are happy to make public.

Our rights

We may decline, edit for clarity, or remove any listing or advert at our discretion — for example if it appears inaccurate, unlawful, offensive or unverifiable. Paid upgrades relate to the listing service itself; once a listing is live, upgrade fees are non-refundable, but listings remain published until the property is sold or you ask us to remove them.

No guarantees

We make no promises about how many enquiries or viewings a listing will receive, or that a property will sell. Information on this site, including the selling guide, is general information and not legal, financial or professional advice.

Privacy policy

What we collect and why

  • Listing details (your name, phone number, property information, photos): to publish your advert and to contact you with buyer enquiries, with your consent. We do not publish your phone number — it is used only so we can pass enquiries to you, and you choose when to share it with a buyer.
  • Proof of ownership (e.g. a council tax bill): to verify listings. Never published, and deleted once your listing is verified.
  • Advertiser details: to run your sponsorship and invoice you.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data, run third-party advertising trackers, or send marketing you haven't asked for. The site uses no cookies beyond any that are strictly necessary for it to function.

Retention and your rights

Listing data is kept while your advert is live and for up to 12 months afterwards, then deleted. You can ask us at any time to correct your details, remove your listing, or delete everything we hold about you — email hello@whypayfees.co.uk and we'll confirm within 7 days. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).
