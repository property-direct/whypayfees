Ten minutes, one message

Send us these eight things

Copy the list below into a WhatsApp or email, fill in your answers, attach your photo(s), and you're done. We'll reply to confirm before anything goes live.

  • 1. Property address — house number, street, postcode (we only publish the street name, never the full number unless you want us to)
  • 2. Asking price — and whether it's fixed, "offers over" or "offers around"
  • 3. The basics — bedrooms, bathrooms, property type, freehold or leasehold
  • 4. A description — 100–200 words in your own voice; what you'd tell a friend about the house. We'll tidy it up for you
  • 5. Your EPC rating — legally required before marketing. No EPC yet? It costs £60–£90 and takes a week; see our guide or the assessor on our services page
  • 6. Photos — 1 free, up to 10 on Boost. Landscape (sideways) photos, taken in daylight, work best
  • 7. Your first name and mobile number — this is what buyers will see and call
  • 8. Proof you own it — a photo of a recent council tax bill or mortgage statement. Never published; it just keeps the site honest

What happens next

You sell it. We cheer you on.

1

Buyers ring you

Take their name and number, ask if they're proceedable (sold? cash? mortgage agreed?), and book a viewing time that suits you.

2

Offers come in

Negotiate directly — you know your bottom line. Once you accept, both sides instruct solicitors and it proceeds like any normal sale.

3

Tell us it's sold

We'll slap a SOLD strip across your listing (very satisfying) and take it down after completion. Read the full selling guide first.
